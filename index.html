Why work with us

Edmonton Freight Solutions is a locally owned warehousing and logistics company built to keep your freight moving.

We’re not a trucking company — and that’s our advantage. With years of hands-on experience in transportation and supply chain operations, we’ve built a strong network of trusted carriers across Canada and internationally. That means competitive freight rates, flexible options, and smart routing strategies tailored to your business.

Cross-docking and warehousing are our core services — not side offerings. We support trucking companies, distributors, manufacturers, and growing businesses with short-term storage, freight consolidation, transloading, and coordinated transportation solutions.

From across town to across the world, we handle the logistics details so you can focus on running your business.
